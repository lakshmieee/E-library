LIBRARY RULES AND REGULATION :


  • Every staff / student of the college is eligible for membership of the Library
  • Identity Card is compulsory for getting access to the library. No Books will be issued without ID card.
  • Identity cards are not transferable. The borrower is responsible for the books borrowed on his/her card.
  • All students should sign the entry register of the Library, before entering.
  • Books borrowing limit: Faculty – 5, Non Teaching staff – 2, Student – 3.
  • Readers are advised not to leave their precious and valuable items like money, passport, credit card etc. at the Property Counter.
  • Silence to be maintained in reading room.
  • All the users are requested to switch off their mobiles or keep it on vibration mode.
  • Strict discipline must be maintained in the Library. Indiscipline may lead to disciplinary action and the library privileges may be withdrawn.
  • The Librarian may recall any book from any member at any time and the member shall return the same immediately.
  • General etiquette are expected from every member of information unit and clientele; as such Singing, Loud Speaking, Shouting, Spitting, Sleeping, Smoking, talking on mobile, etc are strictly prohibited.
  • Magazines and journals must be read only in the library on specific tables and should not be taken to any other reading areas.
  • Reference material should not be taken outside the Library.
  • Show the books and other materials which are being taken out of the library to the staff at the entrance counter.
  • Students are instructed to check the books while borrowing and they will be responsible for any type of damage or mutilation noticed at the time of return.
  • The Librarian may recall any book from any member at any time and the member shall return the same immediately.
